Mission Statment

Our mission is simple: connect resturants and local grocery stores to food banks in order to reduce and eliminate food waste in a more efficient manner. Through our website, donors can send their extra produce to charities, who can use this to provide meals for the hungry.

Inspiration

Food insecurity is one of societyâ€™s fastest-growing concerns. Did you know that in 2019, about 10 million children alone suffered from food insecurity, and this alarming number is projected to increase at rapid rates? Despite producing enough food to sustainably feed everyone, the USA creates over 200 billion pounds of food waste each year. As high school students, we felt the need to take this issue into our own hands and create ScrapSaver, an effective communications platform for the relay of produce and cooked meals between restaurants and hotels to food banks and grocery drives.

Donate Food

We know how much unavoidable waste restaurants produce, and we want to help! ScrapSaver helps connect you to local charities. We will pick up your excess produce, such as carrots or potatoes, and send these over to a local charity.

Receive Food

As a food bank you will be responsible for looking for available products on ScrapSaver that resturants and grocery stores willl post. You can also put out requests for any specifc items that you want.
